Failure Attributes

The failure analysis shows persistent weaknesses in exact counting, compositional prompts with multiple attributes, human and animal structure, uncommon size relationships, perspective control, and text or symbol rendering. These challenges remain visible across model tiers, even as stronger models improve broad object grounding and scene understanding.

Year-wise trends show why alignment-only metrics can overstate progress: newer models often satisfy object and attribute presence better, but still contain visual realism and structural errors that DynEval captures through its IQA branch.
